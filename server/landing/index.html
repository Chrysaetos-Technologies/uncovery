Introducing Privy

Impermanent communication - sharing fleeting thoughts and ideas - has become a popular way to connect with our friends. At the same time, we get a great deal of satisfaction from discovering new things. By combining these thoughts, Privy is a service that provides users with the opportunity to explore their surrounding area, uncover new ideas, and express themselves in an open, anonymous environment.
